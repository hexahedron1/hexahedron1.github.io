(, also known as , ) is a species of sentient cubes with an orange color and no facial features. The only specimen of this species remains near Stavropol, Russia.


This species is characterized by falling behind current technology. Their computers often use an AMD Ryzen 5 3500 CPU, 16 gigabytes of RAM, a Radeon RX 580 graphics card, hard drives, 16:10 monitors and no RGB lighting.
Since late 2024, their computers run exclusively Linux, but the specific distribution may vary. Notable examples include: Linux Mint, Archcraft, Fedora, Debian, Manjaro and Arch, with the predominant desktop environment being KDE Plasma.


Hexahedra often show interest in computers, programming, animation, physics, chemistry, mathematics, constructed languages, Rubik's cubes, electronics, chiptune and video games. Common games include: Minecraft, Noita, Shapez 2, Portal 2, Geometry Dash, Half Life 2 (and episodes).
Interestingly, they does not experience sexual or romantic attraction, even within the species.[citation needed]
Hexahedra show an abnormal interest in CuSO₄.


Hexahedra stay inside their house for the majority of time, and rarely get out. They are mostly active on discord and telegram.
